Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 9, 2026

Flores Property Solutions OC, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or submit a request for a cash offer.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you fill out a form on our website, including your name, email address, phone number, and property details (such as the property address, its condition, your goals for the property, estimated mortgage balance, and desired sale proceeds).

We also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, and interactions with the site, through cookies and similar technologies as described in Section 3.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to evaluate your property, formulate a cash offer, communicate with you regarding your inquiry, respond to your questions, and improve our website and advertising.

3. Cookies, Meta Pixel & Advertising Analytics

Our website uses the Meta Pixel, an analytics and advertising tool provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. (Facebook/Instagram). The Meta Pixel uses cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your visit — such as the pages you view, the actions you take (for example, submitting a form), and technical details about your device and browser. This information helps us measure the effectiveness of our advertising and show our ads to people more likely to be interested in our services.

Information collected by the Meta Pixel is transmitted to Meta and is subject to Meta's Privacy Policy. You can manage how Meta uses your data for ads through your Facebook Ad Preferences, and you can limit tracking generally through your browser's cookie settings or industry opt-out tools such as aboutads.info.

4. How Your Form Information Is Processed

When you submit a form on our website, your submission is transmitted to us using Formspree, a third-party form processing service, solely for the purpose of delivering your inquiry to us. Formspree's handling of this data is governed by its own privacy policy.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We are a private, local investment firm. We may share your information only with service providers who help us operate our business (such as the form processing and advertising analytics services described above), with professionals involved in a potential transaction with your consent (such as title or escrow companies), or where required by law.

6. SMS Consent & Data Sharing

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will strictly not be shared with any third parties.

By submitting our form and checking the consent box, you agree that we may contact you by phone, text message (SMS), and email regarding your property inquiry. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP, and you may reply HELP for assistance. Consent to receive messages is not a condition of any purchase or sale.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, including if you are a resident of Washington or California, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to know what personal information we have collected about you, the right to request that we delete it, and the right to correct inaccurate information. California residents may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA). To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below and we will respond as required by applicable law. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

8. Data Retention & Security

We retain your information for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry, evaluate a potential transaction, and comply with legal obligations. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information; however, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
